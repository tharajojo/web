Daksh 2021


DAKSH is an annual educational and career fair conducted and organized by the Student Council of Christ (Deemed to be University).
As the flagship event of the Student Council, DAKSH aims to provide a platform for all the students to explore the various courses and career options available to them.
The Fair intends to reach the students pursuing their 12th boards and final year of Pre-University/Undergraduate Courses.
